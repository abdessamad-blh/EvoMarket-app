## The 3 Key Platforms for the Moroccan Market

### Facebook & Instagram: The Essential Duo

**Facebook** remains the #1 platform in Morocco by total audience, especially effective for:
- The 25-54 age demographic (the primary SME target)
- Ultra-precise geographic targeting: Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, city by city, neighborhood by neighborhood
- Brand awareness, website traffic, and lead generation objectives

**Instagram** dominates premium segments and the 18-34 demographic — perfect for:
- Lifestyle, fashion, beauty, food, and luxury brands
- High-quality visual content (Reels, sponsored Stories)
- Influencer marketing with Moroccan creators

Our approach: we manage both in sync from **Meta Business Manager**, with creatives adapted to each format and objective.

### Google Ads: Capturing Intent at the Decisive Moment

The fundamental difference between Facebook Ads and Google Ads: **Facebook interrupts** — you show your ad to someone who wasn't looking. **Google responds** — you appear exactly when someone searches for what you sell.

For Morocco, Google Ads is especially powerful for:
- High-intent transactional searches: *"web agency Casablanca price"*, *"plumber Rabat emergency"*, *"English course Marrakech"*
- Shopping campaigns for e-commerce with CMI integration
- Google My Business Ads for immediate local visibility

### TikTok: The Platform Your Competitors Still Ignore

With **8 million Moroccan users** and still-low cost per thousand impressions (CPM) versus Facebook, TikTok represents the asymmetric opportunity of the moment for Moroccan brands.

Businesses positioning there now are capturing a young, engaged audience at a cost Facebook won't allow in 2 years.

## The Complete Digital Marketing Strategy According to EvoMarket

### Phase 1 — Audit and Moroccan Persona Definition

Before spending a single dirham on advertising, we answer precise questions:

- Who is your ideal client in Morocco? (age, city, behaviors, purchasing power)
- What time of day are they most active on each platform?
- What content makes them stop scrolling?
- What objections come up most before purchase?

These answers define your **target audience** and **key message** — the foundation of everything that follows.

### Phase 2 — Content That Converts

Generic content doesn't convert. Here's what works on the Moroccan market in 2026:

**Short video (Reels / TikTok)**: 15 to 60-second videos generate **5 to 10 times more** engagement than static images. Our production team creates professional video content adapted to Moroccan visual culture.

**Authentic client testimonials**: The Moroccan consumer trusts real client reviews far more than classic ads. We integrate real case studies into your ad creatives.

**Bilingual FR/AR content**: Double your reach by creating Arabic versions of your key content. An ad in Moroccan Arabic (Darija) can generate **3 to 4 times more** engagement than French alone for certain targets.

**Educational content**: Explain, inform, bring value before selling — the strategy that builds long-term trust with the Moroccan audience.

### Phase 3 — Precise Targeting and Campaign Launch

We structure your campaigns using the **Full-Funnel** method:

**Top of Funnel (TOFU) — Awareness**:
Broad audiences, engaging content, objective: introduce your brand to prospects who don't know you yet.

**Middle of Funnel (MOFU) — Consideration**:
Retargeting of people who engaged with your content, lookalike audiences of your best clients, objective: nurture interest.

**Bottom of Funnel (BOFU) — Conversion**:
Precise retargeting of site visitors and warm leads, direct offers, objective: sale or contact.

### Phase 4 — Continuous Optimization and Transparent Reporting

Digital marketing is not "launch and forget". We analyze every week:
- **CTR** (Click-Through Rate) per creative and audience
- **CPL** (Cost Per Lead) and **CPA** (Cost Per Acquisition) per campaign
- **ROAS** (Return On Ad Spend) for every dirham invested
- Permanent A/B tests on headlines, visuals, and landing pages

You receive a **transparent monthly report** with real numbers — not vanity metrics.

## Community Management: Your Brand's Voice in Darija and French

A page that posts without responding to comments is a dead page. **Community management** in Morocco has its own codes:

- Respond quickly (ideally within 2 hours) — the Moroccan audience is demanding on this point
- Alternate Darija / French / Arabic depending on context and platform
- Handle negative reviews with diplomacy and professionalism
- Run games, polls, and interactive content to maximize organic reach

Our Community Management team is available 6 days a week to manage your brand's online presence with the tone and culture of the Moroccan market.
