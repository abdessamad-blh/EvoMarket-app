## Why Wix Is Not Enough for Moroccan SEO

### The Bloated Code Problem

Wix auto-generates HTML/CSS/JavaScript that is, technically, a catastrophe for search engines. Wix pages load in an average of **5 to 8 seconds** — on a fiber connection. On a **Moroccan 4G network** during peak hours? Much more.

Google.ma uses *Core Web Vitals* as a direct ranking factor. If your LCP (*Largest Contentful Paint*) exceeds 2.5 seconds, your site is penalized in search results — regardless of the quality of your content.

**A custom-built site** with Next.js hosted on a Morocco-optimized CDN can achieve an LCP of **0.8 to 1.2 seconds**. That's the difference between page 1 and page 3 on Google.ma for "website creation Morocco".

### Moroccan Local SEO Is Complex — Wix Doesn't Understand It

**SEO Morocco** is bilingual by nature. Your potential clients search in French (*"web agency Rabat"*), in Arabic (*"إنشاء موقع إلكتروني المغرب"*), and sometimes in romanized Darija.

Wix cannot intelligently handle:
- Correct **hreflang URLs** for bilingual FR/AR content
- A structured *sitemap* for both languages simultaneously
- Localized Schema.org markup with Moroccan geographic data
- Fine-tuned *meta tags* management per language and region

A professional Moroccan web agency builds a bilingual SEO architecture from the foundation — not as an afterthought.
