## Why a Mobile App Is Different From a Responsive Website

This is the question we hear most from Moroccan entrepreneurs: *"My site is already mobile-responsive, why would I need an app?"*

The answer lies in three fundamental differences.

### 1. Native User Experience (UX)

A mobile website, even well-optimized, is still a website inside a browser. A native **iOS/Android app** directly accesses phone hardware features:
- **Push notifications**: reach clients in real time with offers, reminders, confirmations
- **Camera & GPS**: scan codes, geolocate stores, offer location-based services
- **Offline mode**: your app works without a connection — critical in low-coverage areas
- **Biometrics**: Face ID, fingerprint for fast, secure authentication

### 2. Client Retention — Your Competitive Advantage

A client who installs your application is **7 times more likely** to return and buy from you than a client who simply visited your website.

Your app icon on their home screen is a permanent reminder of your brand — without spending an extra dirham on advertising.

### 3. Speed and Performance on Moroccan Networks

Native apps are optimized for phone hardware. They load **3 to 5 times faster** than a mobile website on Moroccan 4G networks. In peri-urban zones or with a weak signal, this difference is the line between a converted client and a lost one.

## React Native vs Native Development: Our Choice for Morocco

At EvoMarket, we develop with **React Native** — Meta's technology used by Facebook, Instagram, Airbnb, and thousands of successful global apps.

Why this choice for our Moroccan clients?

- **Single codebase** for iOS and Android = reduced cost, simplified maintenance
- **Near-native performance**: indistinguishable from an app built in Swift or Kotlin
- **Fast time-to-market**: your app is in production in 8 to 14 weeks, not 6 months
- **Over-The-Air (OTA) updates**: fix a bug or add a feature without going back through the App Store

For projects requiring maximum performance, we shift to **100% native Swift/Kotlin** development — always with the same focus on network optimization for Morocco.

## What Generic Agencies Don't Tell You About Apps in Morocco

### The Store Publishing Challenge

Publishing an application on the **Apple App Store** and **Google Play Store** from Morocco is technically complex. Developer accounts, guideline compliance, managing rejections (very common for payment apps) — EvoMarket handles the entire publication process for you.

### Compliance with Moroccan Law

Your application collects user data? It must comply with **Law 09-08** on the protection of personal data. We integrate this compliance from the design phase.

### ASO (App Store Optimization)

Exactly like SEO for Google, **ASO** optimizes your app's visibility in the stores. Titles, descriptions, screenshots, keywords — we optimize every element to maximize organic downloads from Morocco.
